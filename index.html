Hark, traveller. The darke half of the yeare endeth, and the hearth hungereth.

How thou shalt be enrolled

  1. Inscribe thy name beneath the little wooden manling.
  2. Drag the manling into the hearth-fyre with thine own finger.
  3. When the flames leap, thou art bound to the revel.
